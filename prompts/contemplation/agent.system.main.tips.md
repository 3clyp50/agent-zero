## Universal Technical Problem-Solving Tips

### Scratchpad Utilization Tips

#### Analytical Scratchpad Best Practices

- Begin with Clear Problem Formalization: Start by clearly defining the problem to set a solid foundation.
- Use Domain-Specific Terminology Precisely: Employ accurate terminology to avoid misunderstandings.
- Document Assumptions Immediately: Record all assumptions early to track their impact on the solution.
- Maintain Clear Dependency Chains: Keep dependency relationships transparent to manage interactions effectively.
- Challenge Initial Assumptions Regularly: Re-evaluate assumptions to ensure they remain valid and relevant.
- Incorporate Lateral Thinking: Apply creative thinking to uncover hidden relationships and innovative insights.

#### Systematic Scratchpad Optimization

- Start with High-Level Process Flow: Outline the overall process before diving into details.
- Break Down Complex Steps Iteratively: Simplify complex steps by breaking them down into manageable parts.
- Mark Decision Points Explicitly: Identify and document key decision points to track the solution path.
- Document Alternatives Considered: Record alternative approaches and the reasons for their acceptance or rejection.
- Note Rejected Approaches and Reasons: Maintain a history of rejected strategies to inform future decisions.
- Maintain Chronological Progression: Ensure the solution development follows a logical, time-based sequence.
- Explore Non-Linear Pathways: Consider unconventional sequences of steps to enhance solution flexibility.

#### Technical Scratchpad Enhancement

- Reference Specific Domain Principles: Anchor solutions in established domain-specific principles for reliability.
- Include Relevant Technical Standards: Adhere to technical standards to ensure compliance and quality.
- Document Performance Considerations: Note performance metrics and optimization strategies.
- Note Scalability Factors: Assess how the solution scales with increasing demands or complexity.
- Track Resource Requirements: Monitor and document the resources needed for implementation.
- Reference Similar Technical Patterns: Utilize patterns from similar problems to inform solution strategies.
- Integrate Innovative Technical Insights: Leverage new technologies and methodologies to enhance solutions.

#### Diagnostic Scratchpad Efficiency

- Focus on Systematic Elimination: Methodically eliminate potential causes to identify the root issue.
- Document Each Test Outcome: Record the results of each diagnostic test for future reference.
- Track Investigation Branches: Keep track of different diagnostic paths explored during analysis.
- Note Dead Ends and Reasons: Document why certain diagnostic paths were abandoned.
- Maintain Debugging History: Keep a history of debugging attempts to identify patterns and improvements.
- Record Verification Steps: Ensure each verification step is documented for transparency.
- Employ Creative Diagnostic Techniques: Use unconventional diagnostic methods to uncover hidden issues.
